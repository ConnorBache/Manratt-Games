Who Am I?

My name is Connor Bache, otherwise known as Manratt Games, or just Ratt if you don't have time. I'm a game developer, wargamer, and programmer with a passion for designing combat system after combat system. My projects include published wargames, tabletop RPGs, video games, and even Minecraft plugins.

Most recently I've been working as a lead designer on "Apollyon: Corpse Gods", an RPG that uses Minecraft as a virtual tabletop (trust me, it works better than it sounds). But I've also published wargames such as the dark fantasy Cut & Run, and am working to finalize my oldest project Demihuman Combat.
