Cut & Run

Cut & Run is a narrative skirmish wargame set in a fantasy medieval period where players manage mercenary warbands aiming to overthrow His Majesty, The King. Designed for 1-8 players, the game emphasizes strategic planning, customizable warbands, and dynamic campaigns. Players engage in battles, manage resources, and navigate a richly woven narrative to achieve their ultimate goal: regicide.
