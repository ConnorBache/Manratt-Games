Apollyon: Corpse Gods

Apollyon: Corpse Gods is a dark fantasy roleplaying experience set in a grim world of forgotten deities, decaying civilization, and eldritch horrors. Players take on the role of those who carry the lingering marks of long-dead gods, navigating treacherous ruins, unraveling ancient secrets, and surviving the dangers lurking beneath the surface.

This Minecraft roleplay server is an immersive, narrative-driven experience, blending combat mechanics, exploration, and player-driven storytelling. Factions clash, the unknown beckons, and the deeper you go, the darker it gets.
